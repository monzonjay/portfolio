About Me

My name is Jay "Shadow" Monzon, I am a proffesional artist, with over 8 years of professional tattoo shop experience and a lifetime of experimentation with many mixed art mediums. I specialize in black and grey photo realistic tattoos. I have worked as a guest and resident artist for tattoo shops all over the country such as; “La Vida Tattoo” in Las Vegas NV, “Fire & Ice Studio” in South Padre Island TX, “Junkyard Ink” (now “Black Flag Collective”) formerly in Louisville CO, “Philadelphia Eddie's” in Philadelphia PA.
